## Day 1 — Nifty SMA Crossover Strategy

**Data:** NSE Nifty 50 (2015–2024)  
**Strategy:** Buy when SMA20 crosses above SMA50, sell when below  
**Backtest Period:** 2020–2024  

| Metric | Strategy | Buy & Hold |
|--------|----------|------------|
| Total Return | 110.3% | 146.6% |
| Sharpe Ratio | 0.82 | 0.77 |
| Total Trades | 19 | 1 |
| Cost per trade | 0.1% | — |

**Key Learning:** Higher Sharpe = better risk-adjusted return.
Strategy avoided COVID crash 2020. Choppy markets (2022) hurt SMA strategies.
Out-of-sample test (2015–2019) showed Sharpe -0.01 — overfitting risk identified.
